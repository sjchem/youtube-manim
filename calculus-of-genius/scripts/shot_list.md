# Shot List: The Calculus of Genius

## Scene 00: Title / Cold Open

- Duration: 15-20 seconds
- Visual: Glowing particles drift, then align into a faint grid.
- Equation: `Creativity approx Attempts x Combinations x Time x Chaos`
- Animation notes: Slow particle convergence, glowing title reveal, equation write-on.
- Voiceover cue: "We like to imagine creativity as lightning..."

## Scene 01: Hook: Creativity Is Not Random

- Duration: 30-35 seconds
- Visual: Lightning strike transforms into probability and entropy symbols.
- Equation: `P(success) = 1 - (1-p)^n`
- Animation notes: Jagged VMobject path, myth/reality panels, camera zoom to equation.
- Voiceover cue: "The myth says genius is a rare accident..."

## Scene 02: Law of Large Numbers

- Duration: 45-50 seconds
- Visual: Lognormal attempt dots cross a breakthrough threshold; probability curve rises.
- Equation: `P(breakthrough) = 1 - (1-p)^n`
- Animation notes: Dots appear one by one, gold outliers, `n` tracker moves from 10 to 1000.
- Voiceover cue: "Most attempts are ordinary..."

## Scene 03: Zipf / Power Law

- Duration: 35-40 seconds
- Visual: Ranked bars drop into a long tail; top bars glow gold.
- Equation: `impact(r) proportional to 1 / r^s`
- Animation notes: Manual bar chart, signal/noise labels, long-tail brace.
- Voiceover cue: "Most ideas vanish. A few survive."

## Scene 04: Combinatorial Creativity

- Duration: 45-55 seconds
- Visual: Concept blocks combine into a new idea; graph/network emerges.
- Equation: `C(n,k) = n! / (k!(n-k)!)`
- Animation notes: Blocks copy into a molecule, optional manim-physics field, combinatorial counts.
- Voiceover cue: "New ideas are rarely born from nothing..."

## Scene 05: Time as Compound Interest

- Duration: 45-50 seconds
- Visual: Flat progress line transforms into exponential growth.
- Equation: `Skill(t) = Skill_0(1+r)^t`
- Animation notes: Traced point, phase labels, daily practice blocks.
- Voiceover cue: "Time is the hidden variable..."

## Scene 06: Edge of Chaos

- Duration: 50-55 seconds
- Visual: Three panels: frozen order, pure chaos, edge-of-chaos attractor flow.
- Equation: `Creativity = Structure x Variation`
- Animation notes: Grid dots, Brownian walkers, attractor particles, entropy slider moves to center.
- Voiceover cue: "Too much order produces repetition..."

## Scene 07: Diminishing Returns vs Breakthrough Risk

- Duration: 30-35 seconds
- Visual: Safe repetition decays; risky exploration spikes.
- Equation: `R_n = R_0(0.7)^n`
- Animation notes: Red decay curve, gold volatile curve, breakthrough flash.
- Voiceover cue: "There is comfort in repeating what worked before..."

## Scene 08: Final Formula / Practical Takeaway

- Duration: 35-40 seconds
- Visual: Previous symbols orbit final equation, then simplify.
- Equation: `Genius = Volume x Remix x Compounding x Controlled Chaos`
- Animation notes: Orbiting icons, TransformMatchingTex, final call-to-action.
- Voiceover cue: "So creativity is not magic..."
