Why does a negative times a negative become positive? In this visual math animation, we move beyond memorizing "minus times minus is plus" and show why the rule is forced by the deeper structure of arithmetic.

We explore multiplication as motion, negative signs as reversals, patterns through zero, and the distributive law proof that makes `(-a)(-b)=ab` unavoidable.
