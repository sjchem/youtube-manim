# Scene-By-Scene Animation Plan

## Scene 01: Cold Open

Duration target: 40-50 seconds

Visual focus: the mysterious equation `(-3)×(-4)=+12`, red negative signs, gold positive result, crossed-out memorization phrase.

Purpose: establish the question and replace rote memory with structural curiosity.

## Scene 02: Multiplication As Motion

Duration target: 50-60 seconds

Visual focus: number line from `-15` to `+15`, glowing particle jumps right for `3×4`, then left for `3×(-4)`.

Purpose: ground multiplication in repeated directed motion.

## Scene 03: Negative Means Reversal

Duration target: 55-65 seconds

Visual focus: arrow labeled `+4`, multiplication by `-1`, 180-degree rotation to `-4`, then a second rotation back.

Purpose: make "negative" feel like an action, not just a symbol.

## Scene 04: The Mirror Room

Duration target: 45-55 seconds

Visual focus: two glowing mirror planes, arrow flips once, then flips again, ending in original orientation.

Purpose: make double reversal memorable.

## Scene 05: Pattern Through Zero

Duration target: 65-75 seconds

Visual focus: animated multiplication table and a straight-line graph. Unknown rows become positive because each product increases by four.

Purpose: show that arithmetic patterns force the sign rule.

## Scene 06: Distributive Law Machine

Duration target: 80-90 seconds

Visual focus: algebra expression enters a machine, distributes, then balances on a scale.

Purpose: prove that `(-3)(-5)=+15` is required by the distributive law.

## Scene 07: Broken Universe

Duration target: 50-60 seconds

Visual focus: assume `(-3)(-5)=-15`, substitute it, show `0≠-30`, then crack the algebraic space.

Purpose: show contradiction if the rule were changed.

## Scene 08: Final Synthesis

Duration target: 55-70 seconds

Visual focus: four panels for motion, reversal, pattern, and structure merging into `(-a)(-b)=ab`.

Purpose: unify intuition and proof into a final satisfying equation.
